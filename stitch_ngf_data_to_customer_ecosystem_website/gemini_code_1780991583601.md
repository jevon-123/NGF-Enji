This unlocks a scalable, high-efficiency sales model where the team focuses only on closing deals.
    - Metrics Achieved:
      * 85% Faster Response Time: Significantly reduces lead and handling time through automated engagement.

      * 10% Conversion Achieved by AI Alone: Conversion rate fully attained by the AI Telemarketer before any human intervention.
